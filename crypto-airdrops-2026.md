## What Is a Crypto Airdrop

A crypto airdrop is a free token distribution used by blockchain projects to attract new users and grow their communities.

Projects launch airdrops to:

- attract early users
- test their products
- increase ecosystem activity
- reward active participants

Sometimes these opportunities can become very valuable.
